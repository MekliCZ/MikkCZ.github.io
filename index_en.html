Who I am

My name is Michal Stanke and you can know me also as Mikk or MikkCZ. I'm a student of FEE CTU in Prague and the Mozilla project contributor.

What I am doing

I have long experience with solving queries on the Mozilla.cz forum where I contribute since 2010, and since 2013 also with real customer support.

In year 2010, I began translating various programs. My first translated program was audio and video converter Format Factory. Then I started to focus on translations for the Mozilla project. In addition to the Instantbird IM client and the BlueGriffon WYSIWYG editor, several add-ons, occasionally also Mozilla Support articles and more. Since 2014 also the Thunderbird e-mail client.

Thanks to the translations, I've got closer to the Mozilla project and became Mozillian. I like the Mozilla mission and would be pleasured to help you to get also involved and support the Web to be free and open.

As for study, I code in Java and C. Also I've met C++, PHP, JavaScript, SQL, Python, VHDL, Bash, Matlab or MIPS. Even if I don't have much to boast with, you can find me on GitHub too.
